Context:
This app is a corporate development / M&A operating portal. It currently tracks the inorganic growth pipeline, opportunities, target details, interactions, actions, stage changes, diligence readiness, import wizard, weekly review, and AI Copilot.

Goal:
Rename and rebrand the visible product as “Ringside” and make the interface feel more distinctive, premium, and memorable.

The name should feel natural in sentences like:
- “Check Ringside before the weekly review.”
- “Log the banker update in Ringside.”
- “Import the tracker into Ringside.”
- “Open Ringside and see which Must-Win assets need attention.”

Brand direction:
Ringside should feel:
- premium
- focused
- executive
- easy to use
- close to the action
- leadership-ready
- grounded in deal execution
- subtly inspired by the idea of being ringside / in the corner, without becoming cheesy or sports-themed

Do NOT add boxing gloves, ring ropes, fighter imagery, or gimmicky sports visuals.
