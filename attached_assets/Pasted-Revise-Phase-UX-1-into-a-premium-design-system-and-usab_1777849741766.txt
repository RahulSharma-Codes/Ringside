Design direction:
Create a premium, executive, Apple-inspired “glass-lite” interface for an M&A / Corporate Development operating system.

The app should feel:
- senior
- calm
- high-trust
- boardroom-ready
- mobile-first
- operational
- not generic
- not overly colorful
- not obviously AI-generated

Core principles:
1. Readability before decoration.
2. Glass effects used sparingly and semantically.
3. Strong hierarchy on every page.
4. Consistent status colors.
5. Mobile-first cards with no horizontal overflow.
6. Accessible contrast on all surfaces.
7. Subtle transitions only.
8. No flashy landing-page styling.

Accessibility requirements:
- Maintain strong contrast.
- Do not place low-contrast text over translucent surfaces.
- Do not rely on color alone for status.
- Make buttons and tap targets comfortable on mobile.
- Verify 375px mobile width has no horizontal overflow.

Specific warnings:
- Do not make every card transparent.
- Do not use rainbow gradients everywhere.
- Do not introduce random new colors per page.
- Do not make the UI look like a crypto dashboard.
- Do not reduce information density so much that it becomes inefficient.
- Do not hide key operating data behind visual effects.
